About Borahae

“Borahae” is a Korean phrase that translates to “I purple you” and is used as a term of endearment and affection. The phrase combines two Korean words: bora (violet) and saranghae (I love you).So as an ARMY(Fan of BTS) i created this library in their name. Here you will find all the books that I love and the books that BTS members recommended and many more books that anyone can read.
